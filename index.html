The Vision

The Indonesian church in Europe
is larger than we think.

Across Germany, Switzerland, Austria, and the Netherlands, Indonesian congregations are growing — but too often in isolation. Without shared resources, shared voices, or shared vision.

GatherID is the first conference of its kind: a gathering to equip and connect Indonesian churches across Europe. Not just an inspiring weekend — but the beginning of something lasting.

⚡
Equipping
Spiritual and practical input for every stage of life — students, young professionals, families, church leaders, and ministry teams. Real tools for real situations.
🤝
Connecting
Structured roundtables and open conversations between churches from across Europe. Building relationships that carry beyond the weekend — toward a shared network and voice.

"Pentecost is the birth of the Church. It is the right moment to begin something together."

— Karlo Röding, Host Pastor, Jemaat Indonesia München

The Program

Built for everyone —
including pastors with Sunday services.

We know: Sunday morning is sacred for every pastor and church. That's why GatherID is deliberately structured so that church leaders can join from Sunday afternoon — and still experience the heart of the conference on Monday.

🧑‍⚖️
A note for pastors & church leaders You lead your own service on Sunday morning — we know that. GatherID is designed with you in mind: arrive Sunday afternoon, settle in, and be fully present for the connecting roundtables and commissioning on Monday. The most important sessions for church leaders are reserved for Sunday evening and Monday.

Main Speaker

A voice that equips.

Main Speaker · GatherID 2027

Jose Carol

Jakarta, Indonesia

Jose Carol is the lead pastor of one of Indonesia's largest and most influential free churches in Jakarta. Known for his ability to bridge deep biblical teaching with practical, life-changing application, he has spoken at conferences and churches across Asia and beyond.

At GatherID 2027, Jose Carol will bring the kind of message that equips — not just inspires. We are honored to welcome him to Munich for this historic first gathering.

More speakers to be announced. We are working with pastors and leaders from across the Indonesian diaspora in Europe. Sign up for the newsletter to be the first to know.
